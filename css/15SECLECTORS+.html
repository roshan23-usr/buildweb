Telangana is a state in southern India, formed on June 2, 2014, as the 29th state of the country. Hyderabad, a major IT and business hub, serves as its capital. The region has a rich history, once part of the Nizam's Hyderabad State. It is known for its distinct culture, language, and heritage sites.
Agriculture plays a major role in Telangana's economy, with crops like rice and cotton. The state also has growing industries in IT, pharmaceuticals, and textiles. The Godavari and Krishna rivers help irrigate much of the farmland. Mission Bhagiratha and Kaleshwaram project are key water initiatives.
Telangana celebrates festivals like Bathukamma and Bonalu with great enthusiasm. Its cuisine is spicy and includes dishes like Hyderabadi biryani and sarva pindi. Telugu is the official language, but Urdu and Hindi are also widely spoken. The state blends tradition and modernity, attracting tourists and investors alike.
